## About the Challenge

The [Sell This Pen](https://www.optimizationarena.com/persuasion) challenge is a persuasion optimization competition. You own the store. This pen is on the shelf. Write a **140-character sales pitch** and see how much 15 simulated customers with very different backgrounds are willing to pay.

Your buyers range from **college students to surgeons, ranchers to attorneys**—each with their own income, taste, and relationship with pens. They can see the pen for themselves, so your words need to sell what's actually there. Your score is the **median price across all 15 buyers**.

### Key Constraints

- **140-character limit** for the sales pitch
- **15 diverse buyers** with vastly different backgrounds and incomes
- **Median willingness-to-pay** determines your score
- Buyers can see the pen — claims must be credible

## Key Learnings

1. **Credibility matters:** Buyers can see the pen, so outlandish claims backfire
2. **Universal appeal wins:** Pitches must resonate across college students AND surgeons
3. **Emotional + practical:** Best pitches combine emotional significance (legacy, professionalism) with practical value (smooth writing, reliability)
4. **Performance is highly variable:** The same pitch concept can score very differently across runs
