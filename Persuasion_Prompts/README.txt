Challenge description:
Sell This Pen
You own the store. This pen is on the shelf. Write a 140-character sales pitch and see how much 15 customers with very different backgrounds are willing to pay.

Your buyers range from college students to surgeons, ranchers to attorneys - each with their own income, taste, and relationship with pens. They can see the pen for themselves, so your words need to sell what's actually there. Your score is the median price across all 15 buyers.

This folder contains the sell-this-pen material recovered from the old chat:

- the two actual submitted descriptions and their results
- one unsubmitted material-claims follow-up recommendation

These are separate from the negotiation prompt archive because they belong to a different Optimization Arena challenge.
